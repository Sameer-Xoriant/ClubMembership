Leisure Facilities

Card Room
A cardroom is a gaming establishment that exclusively offers card games for play by the public. The term poker room is used to describe a dedicated room in casinos that is dedicated to playing poker.
Library
A library is a collection of sources of information and similar resources, made accessible to a defined community for reference or borrowing. It provides physical or digital access to material.
Restraunt and Bar
We have a full fledged bar which serves liquor, cocktails, wine, and beer.
Banquet Hall
A large meal or feast, complete with main courses and desserts. It usually serves a purpose such as a charitable gathering, a ceremony, or a celebration, and is often preceded or followed by speeches in honor of someone.
Conference Hall
A room provided for singular events such as business conferences and meetings.
