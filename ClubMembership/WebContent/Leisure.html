Leisure Facilities

Card Room
A card room is a gaming room that exclusively offers card games for play by the public of the club.
Library
A library is a collection of sources of information and similar resources, made accessible to a defined community for reference or borrowing.
Restaurant and Bar
We have a full fledged bar which serves liquor, cocktails, wine, and beer.
Banquet Hall
A large meal or feast, complete with main courses and desserts. It usually serves a purpose such as a charitable gathering, a ceremony, or a celebration.
Conference Hall
A room provided for singular events such as business conferences and meetings.
